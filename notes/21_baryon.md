#### baryon number
The conservation of the baryon number is a fundamental principle in physics, playing a critical role in the stability of protons, the lightest of the baryons. 
However, an alternative theory proposed in the early 1970s suggests that the valence quarks are connected in a Y-shaped structure known as gluon-junction or baryon-junction Artru (1975); Rossi and Veneziano (1977); Kharzeev (1996) (see Fig. 1 (b)). 
This junction is assumed to be the gauge-invariant structure that carries the baryon number Suganuma et al. (2005); Takahashi et al. (2001).
